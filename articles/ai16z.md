# ai16z

**ai16z** is a decentralized autonomous organization ([[DAO]]) focused on AI and crypto infrastructure, best known as the creator of [[ElizaOS]], one of the most popular open-source frameworks for building autonomous [[AI agents]]. The organization operates as a crypto-native AI venture entity, combining DAO governance with AI development and investment activities.

## Overview

ai16z emerged in 2024 as part of the growing intersection between artificial intelligence and cryptocurrency. The name playfully references [[Andreessen Horowitz]] (a16z), the prominent venture capital firm, though ai16z has no official affiliation with them—a common pattern in crypto culture where projects adopt recognizable naming conventions.

The organization pursues several interconnected objectives:
- Developing open-source AI agent infrastructure
- Investing in AI and crypto projects through DAO treasury
- Building community around autonomous agent technology
- Advancing the "agentic economy" thesis

## ElizaOS

ai16z's primary contribution is [[ElizaOS]], an open-source TypeScript framework for creating autonomous AI agents. The framework enables:

- Multi-platform deployment (Discord, Telegram, Twitter, [[Farcaster]])
- Blockchain integration for crypto-native agents
- Persistent memory and personality systems
- Multi-agent orchestration

ElizaOS has achieved significant adoption with 35,000+ GitHub stars and active deployment across DeFi, social media, and other domains. The project was formally documented in an academic paper published on arXiv in January 2025.

## Philosophy

ai16z embodies several key principles:

### Open Source First
All core infrastructure is MIT licensed and publicly available. This contrasts with proprietary AI agent platforms and reflects crypto's open-source ethos.

### Crypto-Native AI
Rather than adapting traditional AI for blockchain, ai16z builds AI systems designed from the ground up for crypto environments—agents that can hold wallets, sign transactions, and participate in DeFi.

### Community Ownership
The DAO structure ensures that the community, not a corporation, controls the direction of development. Contributors can participate in governance and share in ecosystem growth.

### Agentic Future
ai16z bets on a future where autonomous AI agents are primary economic actors, necessitating infrastructure that treats agents as first-class participants rather than tools.

### Relationship with Virtuals Protocol

While [[Virtuals Protocol]] focuses on agent tokenization and economic coordination, ai16z/ElizaOS provides the underlying framework many Virtuals agents use. The two projects are complementary rather than competitive.
